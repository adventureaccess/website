High in the Indian Himalaya, beyond India's green plains, lies the hidden region of Ladakh — a living crossroads of Himalayan culture, rugged and remote to a degree few travelers ever experience.

01 — Terrain & culture

Adventure and culture, perfectly blended.

Years of relationships across Ladakh and Zanskar let us design journeys that balance discovery with respect — hidden-valley treks, freeride MTB descents from high passes, warm and authentic homestays.

Our routes follow trails once used by traders and villagers. Every itinerary blends active exploration with relational depth: family visits, traditional meals, village festivals.

02 — Your journey, your way

No two travelers are alike. No two itineraries either.

Combine a high-altitude trek with gentle monastery days, or link an overland Jeep route with mountain biking in Zanskar. Tell us what speaks to you — challenge, immersion, or solitude — and we shape the journey around your group.

03 — Meaningful relationships

Leave changed, not just photographed.

Our guides and partners are locals and regional experts who make sure every journey benefits the communities you visit and the fragile ecosystems you explore. You'll leave Ladakh not just with memories — changed for the better.

When to go

Passes open and trails clear from mid-May through mid-October. Summer brings clear skies, warm days, and access to Zanskar's remote valleys.
